Who We Are?


Āyubōwan! (May you live long)
Welcome to Yātrā!
On this resplendent isle, we wish all our guests a long life. And even though some have called it paradise, we call it home. Here at Yātrā we help our guests to explore the best of what this paradise could offer. Started as a college project in 2022, now Yātrā is funded by the Ceylon Tourism Board. Yātrā has been recommended by various diplomatic institutions such as the United States Embassy of Sri Lanka & Maldives, British High Commission, French Embassy of Sri Lanka, European Commission of Sri Lanka, Embassy of Japan in Sri Lanka, Embassy of Republic of Korea & Australian High Commission.
Note: WE DO NOT SUPPORT ANY CHINESE, RUSSIAN, BELARUSSIAN OR IRANIAN NATIONALITIES WHO ARE WISHING TO TRAVEL IN SRI LANKA. PARDON US. BUT IT'S NOT US, IT'S YOU. AND YES, WE'RE PRO-DEMOCRATIC. :)
For nearly 13 years, we have been honoured to share the magic of our tropical land with the world.
Be it hidden amidst the mystique of historical cities, nestled away in rustic villages, cushioned warmly up in the mountain clouds, or pampered down by the setting sun of the Indian Ocean - we will guide you, wherever you may find fancy yourself in Sri Lanka.
The world has graced our shores for centuries, forging a beautiful relationship that has always held a special place in our hearts. So special in fact, that even though you may arrive as guests, you will leave as friends, but return as family. Because when you stay with us, you stay at the home of hospitality.

We look forward to meeting you.

Supun Wijesooriya
Chairman, Yātrā.
